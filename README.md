#  Vishal Mega Mart Annual Sales Report Dashboard (Excel Project)

##  Project Overview
This project presents an interactive sales analysis dashboard for **Vishal Mega Mart Annual Report 2022**. The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales performance, customer demographics, order status, channel performance, and regional sales distribution.

The objective of this project is to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights that help stakeholders understand customer behavior, identify top-performing sales channels, and make data-driven decisions.

---

##  Key Objectives

- Analyze monthly sales and order trends.
- Compare purchasing behavior between men and women.
- Monitor order status (Delivered, Cancelled, Returned, Refunded).
- Identify top-performing states based on sales.
- Evaluate the effectiveness of different sales channels.
- Analyze customer age and gender distribution.
- Enable dynamic filtering through interactive slicers.

---

##  Dashboard Features

### 1. Order vs Sales Analysis
- Monthly comparison of total sales and order count.
- Identifies seasonal trends and peak sales periods.

### 2. Gender-wise Customer Distribution
- Breakdown of orders placed by Men and Women.
- Helps understand target customer demographics.

### 3. Order Status Tracking
- Delivered Orders
- Cancelled Orders
- Returned Orders
- Refunded Orders

### 4. Top 5 States by Sales
- Highlights regions generating the highest revenue.
- Useful for regional business strategy.

### 5. Channel Performance Analysis
Sales contribution from:
- Amazon
- Flipkart
- Myntra
- Ajio
- Meesho
- Nalli
- Others

### 6. Age vs Gender Analysis
- Customer segmentation based on age groups and gender.
- Helps identify the most active customer segments.

##  Tools & Technologies Used

- Microsoft Excel (Data Cleaning & Preparation)
- Power BI (Data Visualization & Dashboard Development)
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- Data Modeling

---

##  Key Insights

- Women contributed a higher percentage of total orders compared to men.
- Amazon emerged as the leading sales channel.
- Maharashtra generated the highest sales revenue among all states.
- Most orders were successfully delivered with minimal cancellations and returns.
- Adult customers represented the largest purchasing segment.
